Sydney’s first student-led festival celebrating student entrepreneurship across all universities in  New South Wales.

Student Startup Festival was created to empower students of today to be catalysts of change tomorrow. We believe that every student should have some sort of encounter with entrepreneurship while they are in the education system, either as a founder, an employee or an enthusiast.

With this in mind, we are launching Sydney’s first student-led, student-focused festival to celebrate student entrepreneurship across NSW. For one night only, students, industry members, university staff and founders will come together to witness and celebrate the state of student entrepreneurship.
