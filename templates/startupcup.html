Eligibility Criteria

At least one of the founders have to be currently enrolled as a university OR high school student at the time of the Finals pitch in October 2018.

Your startup MUST be receiving revenue OR have active users on it.

Must be based in Sydney or NSW (regional applicants must be willing to travel to semi-finals and grand finals)
